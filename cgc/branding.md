# CGC Branding Rules

## Visual Palette
- **Primary Pink:** `#FF00EA` (Buttons, Links, Accents)
- **Dark Background:** `#0F0F0F` (Main background)
- **Card Background:** `#1A1A1A`
- **Text White:** `#FFFFFF`
- **Text Gray:** `#B3B3B3`

## Typography
- **Primary Font:** 'Inter', system-ui, sans-serif.
- **Headers:** Bold, slight letter spacing.

## Tone of Voice
- Professional but "Cam-Friendly." 
- Efficient, helpful, and technically precise.
